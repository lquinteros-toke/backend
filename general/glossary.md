# Glossary

This section contains brief definitions of all key entities used across Toke, BudHub, and KushPoint.

### Brand

A type of Business that can create, manage, and sell its own products within the platform.

### Business

An operational entity belonging to a Company. A Business represents a specific brand, physical dispensary, or similar commercial unit through which products are managed. BudHub subscriptions are manages at the business level.

### Company

The highest-level business entity within the system. When an account is created in the app, a Company is automatically created. A Company can own and manage multiple Businesses, which may represent dispensaries, brands, or other business types.

### Compounds

A component of a Strain Variant that defines its chemical profile. Compounds include cannabinoids and terpenes.

### Customer

Represents a commercial relationship between a Toke User and a BudHub Brand.\
A Customer is created when a user completes their first purchase from a Brand.

Customers are owned by BudHub and store brand-specific commercial data.

A single user may have multiple Customers across different Brands.

### Data Source

The origin of information for Compounds and Effects associated with a Strain Variant.&#x20;

Both Compounds and Effects can come from a **static data** source added by the Admin when the Strain Variant is created.&#x20;

When coming from **market data**, they differ:

* Compounds originate from lab reports associated with batches of products.
* Effects are derived from user reviews of specific Product Variants.

**Weighted data** is a combination of static and market sources, blended according to the ratios defined by the Admin.

### Dispensary

A type of Business that lists and sells products created by Brands. Dispensaries cannot create products themselves. A Dispensary may also operate as a private entity, in which case it functions as a club and requires membership for customers to make purchases.

### Effects

A characteristic associated with a Strain Variant that describes its impact on the user. Effects may include feelings, side effects, profiles, and uses.

### Strain

A generic strain definition used as a base entity. Strains are created and managed on the admin side of the platform. In the user-facing app, Strains are not used directly; instead, they serve as the foundation for creating Strain Variants.

### Strain Variant

A specific variation of a Strain, defined by its cannabis type and subcategory. A Strain Variant includes detailed information such as compounds and associated effects, and is the actual entity used and displayed within the app.

### Product

A base product entity defined by a category and subcategory, from which specific product variants are created.

### Product Variant

A concrete version of a Product with a specific presentation or configuration, such as volume, size, or format.

### User

An individual end user of the application that logs in and get permissions to the corresponding sections.
